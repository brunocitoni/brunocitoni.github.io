How To Use:

If not on mobile, click on Save DB and edit the file that you just downloaded. Then click on choose file, choose your edited text file, then click upload file.

You can enter each player's name and value in the boxes on the left and click Add player to list to add players manually.

Once the players list is displayed, tick the checkbox next to each of those you are playing (must be an even number) then select Find Teams!

If successful you will see the dropdown box next to Choose a team populated with values. Choose one and click Display Team
